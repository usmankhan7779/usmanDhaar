How It Works

Seller, Buyer and Store Owners has to sign into Dhaar.pk or download the application via Android or iOS market.



Selling at Fixed Price

A seller lists an item on Dhaar.pk, from fashion stuff, gadgets to home appliances by logging into Dhaar. The seller chooses to accept only bids for the item (in auction category) or to offer the Buy It option, which allows buyers to purchase the item straight away at a fixed price.

In a “Buy It” category, the first buyer willing to pay the seller's price gets the item.



Bidding and Winning:

In an online auction, the bidding opens at a price the seller specifies and remains on Dhaar for an allocated number of days. Buyers then place bids on the item. When the time ends, the buyer with the highest bid wins.



Sale by Major Stores

Dhaar has products on sale by the hundreds of online Pakistani brands to choose from.

In this case, in “Buy It” category, the buyer simply has to pay the price of the product set by the stores.
